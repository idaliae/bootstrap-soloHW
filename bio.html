Thor and Loki: Biography

Thor and Loki were street cats recused from San Antonio, Tx, in 2017.   Thor is the siamese cat. He is not afraid of meeting people and loves attention; just like the Thor in The Avengers.   Loki is the tuxedo cat who loves to play but only with people he knows. He is the definition of "scaredy cat," kind of like the Loki from the movies.
